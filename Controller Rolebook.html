You dictate the flow of battle, not through brawn or firepower, but through cunning. You weaken the foe and strengthen your allies, winning battles without bloodying your hands. You are not a great warrior, but you are a skilled strategist and someone who’s abilities let you get things done.

You will need to pick a name for your Controller. Then pick their pronouns. Then choose one from each line below or write your own:

Hair: Grey hair, charm-knotted hair, unkempt and messy hair, hair

Capricious Fortunes: You can spend TEN on a Cooldown roll. However, you get a Complication on a 1 or 2 on all rolls you make.
Favoured Companion: Choose another character on your team. Any time you are in Short range of them, they get a Perk on a 3 or 4 whenever they roll.
Regeneration: At the start of your turn, you restore 1 Vitality.

Abilities
You start the game with two abilities from the list below:

 Chronosnare (cooldown 8+ ): You place a chronosnare field that affects all hostile characters within Short range. While the snare is active, opposing characters must use two actions to make a single move actions. This effect ends after two rounds.
 Corrode (cooldown 5+ ): Choose a Champion in Short range. Until the end of the next round, their Armour is reduced by 2 and they lose the benefits of any Defensive qualities their weapons have.
 Crumble: Roll UT against a Fortification’s Defence. If you succeed, it loses 1 armour.
 Inspiring Presence: Friendly Goons in Short Range have all rolls Pulled Forwards once.
 Intimidating Presence: Hostile Goons in Short Range have all rolls Set Back once.
 Restore: Use an action to choose a non-Fortification character in short range, then roll UT with a difficulty vs their missing Vitality. On a success, restore 3 Vitality. On a success with a Perk, you may use it to restore 4 Vitality.
 Shutdown: As a Risky Action, make a UT roll vs the targets WIL, with Short range. If the target fails, they cannot use any of their abilities, and lose any benefits from them, until the end of your next turn.
 Summon (Cooldown 8+ ): Use two actions to summon up a minion to fight for you. It uses Melee Goon AI and the Standard Guardian profile.
 Sapping Strike (cooldown 6+ ): Make a UT roll against the REF of another champion within Short range. If you succeed, all STR and SHO rolls they make are Set Back once, and they lose 1 Vitality whenever they take an action. At the end of each of those champion’s turns, they may make a Difficulty 7 FORT roll to cancel this ability’s effect.
 Stasis (cooldown 8+ ): Choose an enemy hero in Short range. Then, as a Risky Action, roll UT vs their WIL. If you succeed, they cannot take any actions in their next turn, and rolls made to attack them are Pulled Forward twice.
 Trap: Use an action to place a trap in your current Zone. The first time an enemy enters this Zone, they must roll REF against your UT. If they fail, the trap deals 7 damage to them. You may have up to 3 traps active at any one time; placing a new one when 3 are down will cancel your earliest trap.
